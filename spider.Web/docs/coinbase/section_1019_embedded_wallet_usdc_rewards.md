# Embedded Wallet USDC Rewards
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/embedded-wallets/usdc-rewards



## Overview

US-based developers can earn 3.35% USDC Rewards on balances held across CDP Embedded Wallets across supported EVM chains including Base, Ethereum-mainnet, Arbitrum, Polygon, Optimism, and Avalanche.

Rewards are available to US-based individuals and businesses who maintain an average daily balance of at least 1 USDC in their CDP Wallets over the course of a given week. Rewards are paid in USDC each week to the developer's Coinbase (Retail or Business) account.

<Tip>
  To receive USDC Rewards, you must have previously held a USDC balance in your Coinbase (Retail or Business) account.
</Tip>

## USDC rewards details

* USDC Rewards is a loyalty program funded by Coinbase. It’s a reward for using Coinbase services to store USDC.
* USDC rewards are available for balances held in CDP Wallets on any supported network.
* Coinbase has no right to use any USDC in your CDP Embedded Wallets.
* We reserve the right to change or discontinue USDC Rewards at any time by reasonable means of notice.
* USDC Rewards are intended for the developer, and may not be marketed toward or used to incentivize a developer's end-users.
