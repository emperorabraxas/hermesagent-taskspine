# Prime Overview
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/prime/concepts/overview



Coinbase Prime offers robust APIs for institutional trading, custody, and staking. These APIs are purpose-built to support integration of Crypto-as-a-Service (CaaS), payments, and trading infrastructure into your applications.

Concepts pages provide foundational guidance on core functionality and integration workflows to accelerate your development process.
