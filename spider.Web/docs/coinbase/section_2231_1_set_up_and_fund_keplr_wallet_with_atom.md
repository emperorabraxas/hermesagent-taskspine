# 1. Set Up and Fund Keplr Wallet with ATOM
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/staking/staking-delegation-guides/cosmos/staking-with-keplr/set-up-and-fund-staking-keplr



## 1. Create a Keplr wallet

1. Navigate to the [Keplr Wallet extension](https://www.keplr.app/download) in the Google Chrome store and follow the on-screen prompts to add the extension to your web browser.
2. Open the browser extension and select *Create new account*.

<Info>
  To use a Ledger hardware device to manage your Keplr wallet, first ensure you have the Cosmos app open and unlocked on your Ledger device. Select ***Import Ledger*** in the Keplr UI and follow the on-screen prompts to connect your Ledger to Keplr.
</Info>

3. Copy your mnemonic seed phrase and store it somewhere safe

<Warning>
  Ensure that you store your mnemonic seed phrase somewhere safe where it cannot be lost or compromised. Your mnemonic seed phrase is the master key to your wallet; loss or compromise of your mnemonic seed phrase may result in permanent loss of your ATOM.
</Warning>

4. Establish an account name and password, then select *Next*.
5. Confirm your mnemonic seed phrase and select *Register*.

## 2. Fund Keplr wallet

6. Open the Keplr wallet browser extension and select *Deposit*.
7. Copy the wallet address that appears.
8. Navigate to the exchange or wallet where your Cosmos ATOMs are stored and send your ATOMs to the Keplr wallet address you just copied. Your assets should now appear in your Keplr wallet.
