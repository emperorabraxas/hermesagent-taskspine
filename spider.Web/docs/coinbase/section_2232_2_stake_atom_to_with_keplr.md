# 2. Stake ATOM to with Keplr
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/staking/staking-delegation-guides/cosmos/staking-with-keplr/stake-atom-to-with-keplr



## 1. Stake to our public validator

1. Navigate to the [Keplr wallet dashboard](https://wallet.keplr.app/).
2. Find the Cosmos Hub chain and click **Stake** to open the [Keplr staking page](https://wallet.keplr.app/chains/cosmos-hub).
3. Find the **Coinbase02** validator and click **Manage**.

<Info>
  **COINBASE VALIDATOR INFORMATION**
  Validator name: Coinbase02
  Validator address : `cosmosvaloper1gqem6t55xrjc8nqf7y8jyp9xzgnm60zu007nrl`
</Info>

4. Click **Delegate**.
5. Enter the amount of ATOM you would like to stake with Coinbase.
6. Click **Delegate**.
7. Approve the transaction in the Keplr wallet extension window that appears.

<Tip>
  **Congratulations! You are now staked to a Coinbase validator.**
</Tip>
