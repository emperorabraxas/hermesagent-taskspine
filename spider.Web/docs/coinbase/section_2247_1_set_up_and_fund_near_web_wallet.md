# 1. Set Up and Fund NEAR Web Wallet
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/staking/staking-delegation-guides/near/web-wallet/set-up-and-fund-near-web-wallet



## 1. Create NEAR web wallet account

1. Navigate to the [NEAR web wallet UI](https://wallet.near.org/).
2. Select *Create Account*.
3. Create a NEAR Account ID, which will be used as your public NEAR address.

Choose your preferred key storage and account recovery method, and follow the on-screen prompts to finalize your account.

<Warning>
  Ensure that you store your Secure Passphrase somewhere safe where it cannot be lost or compromised. Your Secure Passphrase is the master key to your wallet; loss or compromise of your Secure Passphrase may result in permanent loss of your NEAR.
</Warning>

4. Choose your preferred account recovery method.

## 2. Finalize account by funding with NEAR

1. Choose your preferred method to fund your account.

<Info>
  If you recently purchased NEAR from an exchange, select **Manual deposit** and copy the one-time address that appears. Visit the exchange where your NEAR is stored and send your NEAR to the address you just copied.

  If you have an existing .near account, select **Fund with an existing account** and enter your existing .near account information.
</Info>

2. Follow the on-screen prompts to finalize your account creation.
