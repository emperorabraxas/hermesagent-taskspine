# Wallet choices for Polkadot
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/staking/staking-delegation-guides/polkadot/delegation-guide/wallet-choices-for-polkadot



Currently, staking is available using the Polkadot UI, or on Ledger Live with a Ledger S or X hardware device. Be sure to claim your DOT from the DOT Allocation Indicators purchased before June 30, 2020 on Ethereum. If you need help claiming your tokens, follow this guide.

<Info>
  If you are a Coinbase Prime customer, you can delegate your DOT in one-click from your Coinbase Prime dashboard. To learn more, contact your Coinbase Prime account manager.
</Info>
