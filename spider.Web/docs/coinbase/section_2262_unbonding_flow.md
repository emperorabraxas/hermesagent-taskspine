# Unbonding Flow
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/staking/staking-delegation-guides/polygon/unbonding-flow



1. Go to [Polygon Wallet](https://wallet-dev.polygon.technology/staking/).

2. Make sure your wallet is connected (Connect a Wallet).

3. Click My Account. Your staked MATIC displays under "Your Delegations".

4. Click Unbond for the validator you want to unbond from.

   a. Select how much of your stake you would like to unbond (25%, 50%, 75%, or 100% of stake).

   b. Click Confirm Unbond.

   c. Confirm the transaction on your self-custody wallet extension or pop-up. You will see an "Unbonding Initiated" confirmation message.

   Your funds will be returned after the unbonding period of \~9 days is complete.
