# 2. Stake to with Phantom
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/staking/staking-delegation-guides/solana/phantom/stake-with-phantom



## 1. Stake to our public validator

1. Click on your Solana balance in the token list in the Phantom browser extension.

2. Select **Start earning SOL**.

3. Search for **Coinbase** in the search bar of the validator selection screen and select the Coinbase validator.

4. Enter the amount of SOL you would like to stake and select **Stake**.
