# Staking SOL with SolFlare on Ledger
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/staking/staking-delegation-guides/solana/solflare/staking-with-solflare



[SolFlare](https://solflare.com/) is available on mobile, desktop, and as a browser extension. Before beginning these steps, you will need to have [the Solana app installed on your Ledger S or X](https://support.ledger.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016265659-Solana-SOL-?docs=true) hardware wallet device and be using either Chrome or an equivalent browser.

If you do not have any SOL on your Ledger, start at [1: Set Up and Fund SolFlare Wallet](/staking/staking-delegation-guides/solana/solflare/setup-and-fund-solafare). If you already have SOL on your Ledger, proceed to [2: Stake to Coinbase with SolFlare](/staking/staking-delegation-guides/solana/solflare/stake-solflare).
