# Accounts
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/api-reference/payment-apis/rest-api/accounts-under-development/accounts-under-development



The Accounts APIs enable developers to create and manage accounts for themselves (Entity) or for their Customers. An Account is a container that holds assets and can be used for transacting. Accounts can be of different types including entity accounts, customer accounts, prime accounts, and business accounts.
