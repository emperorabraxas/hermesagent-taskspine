# Get Trade Finance Tiered Pricing Fees
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/api-reference/prime-api/rest-api/financing/get-trade-finance-tiered-pricing-fees

GET /v1/entities/{entity_id}/tf_tiered_fees
Get trade finance tiered pricing fees for a given entity at a specific time, default to current time.

### Supported Products

* Trade Finance
