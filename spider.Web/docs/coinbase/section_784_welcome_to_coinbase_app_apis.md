# Welcome to Coinbase App APIs
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/coinbase-app/introduction/welcome



Coinbase App APIs let you programmatically trade, transfer, and track Coinbase accounts, assets, and transactions.

| Trade                                                               | Transfer                                       | Track                                          |
| :------------------------------------------------------------------ | :--------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| Trade on spot and derivatives markets powered by **Advanced Trade** | Transfer funds between accounts and portfolios | Track assets, accounts, transactions, and more |

With Coinbase App APIs, you can:

* Trade on spot and derivatives markets with [Advanced Trade](/coinbase-app/advanced-trade-apis/overview)
* Build [onchain payments](/coinbase-app/transfer-apis/send-crypto) solutions
* Enable Coinbase users to securely sign in to your app using OAuth
* Integrate Coinbase [supported cryptocurrencies](https://help.coinbase.com/en/supported-crypto) and prices, real-time & historical
