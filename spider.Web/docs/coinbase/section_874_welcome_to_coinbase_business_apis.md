# Welcome to Coinbase Business APIs
Source: https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/coinbase-business/introduction/welcome



Coinbase Business APIs let you programmatically make payments, trade, transfer, and track Coinbase Business accounts, assets, and transactions.

Sign up for a Coinbase Business account [**here**](https://www.coinbase.com/business).

With Coinbase Business APIs, you can:

* **Gain real-time visibility into your digital assets:** APIs to view and manage all your Coinbase Business account balances in one place.
* **Automate your crypto trading:** Buy, sell, and trade programmatically on the most trusted and secure crypto exchange.
* **Integrate seamless crypto payments** into your application to power global payouts, collect payments and more.
* **Automate your onramps and offramps:** Easily move your money between local currencies and stablecoins.
